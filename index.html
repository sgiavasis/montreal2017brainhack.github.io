Registration for the 2019 New York City Hackathon is now open! Hurry, seats are limited.

The 2019 NYC Hackathon is an official satellite event of the International Global Brainhack 2019.
The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science and engineering projects in neuroimaging.


Dates: Nov 13-15, 2019

Location: The Child Mind Institute

101 East 56th St., New York City, NY


On-site breakfast, lunch, and coffee provided



Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to www.brainhack.org and to submit a report describing their project to the 2019 Brainhack Proceedings that will be published by Gigascience after the event.

Please join the Brainhack Mattermost project for updated information on the developing Hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas.
