Registration for the 2018 New York City Hackathon is now open! Hurry, seats are limited.

The 2018 NYC Hackathon is an official satellite event of the International Global Brainhack 2018.
The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science and engineering projects in neuroimaging.


Dates: May 2-4, 2018

Location: The Child Mind Institute

101 East 56th St., New York City, NY


On-site breakfast, lunch, and coffee provided



Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to www.brainhack.org and to submit a report describing their project to the 2018 Brainhack Proceedings that will be published by Gigascience after the event.

Please join the Brainhack Slack project for updated information on the developing Hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas.
